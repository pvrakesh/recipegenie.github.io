About RecipeGenie

RecipeGenie is an iOS app designed to help you discover, save, and create delicious recipes with ease.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new recipe?

To create a new recipe, tap the "+" button on the home screen and follow the prompts to enter your recipe details.

Can I import recipes from websites?

Yes! Use the "Import" feature in the app to paste a URL and automatically import recipe details.
