About RecipeGenie

RecipeGenie is an iOS app designed to help you discover, save, and create delicious recipes with ease.

How to Download and Install RecipeGenie

  1. Open the App Store on your iOS device.
  2. Tap the Search icon (magnifying glass) at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Type "RecipeGenie" into the search bar and tap Search.
  4. Look for the RecipeGenie app in the search results and tap on it.
  5. Tap the "Get" or price button next to the app.
  6. Authenticate the download with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
  7. Wait for the app to download and install automatically.
  8. Once installed, tap "Open" to launch RecipeGenie.

Note: Make sure your device is running iOS 16.4 or later, and you have a stable internet connection for the download.
