RecipeGenie Privacy Policy

Introduction

At RecipeGenie, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information in connection with our iOS app, RecipeGenie.

Information Collection and Use

RecipeGenie is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or retrieve any user information beyond what is strictly necessary for the operation of a subscription-based app in the iOS App Store.

User Tracking

App Tracking Transparency

Our app [Your App Name] ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to transparency regarding the collection and use of your data. In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, we may request your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites.

What is tracking?

Tracking refers to the act of linking user or device data collected from our app with user or device data collected from other companies' apps, websites, or offline properties for targeted advertising or advertising measurement purposes. It also refers to sharing user or device data with data brokers.

How we use tracking data

If you allow tracking, we may use this data for the following purposes:

  1. Personalized Advertising: To show you ads that are more relevant to your interests.
  2. Analytics: To better understand how users interact with our app and improve our services.
  3. Attribution: To measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Data collected for tracking

The types of data we may collect and use for tracking include:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA)
  • Device information
  • Usage data

Your choices

You have control over whether our app can track your activity. When you first launch our app, you will be presented with a prompt asking for your permission to track. You can choose to allow or deny this permission.

You can also change your choice at any time in your device's settings:

  1. Go to your device's Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap on [Your App Name]
  3. Toggle "Allow Tracking" on or off

If you opt out

If you choose to opt out of tracking, we will not track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. However, you may still receive ads within our app, but they may be less relevant to your interests.
